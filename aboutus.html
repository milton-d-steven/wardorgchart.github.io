About Us

Company: Called to Serve, LLC
Site Administrator: Steven Milton
Last Updated: 3/25/2019

Mission: The mission of this site is to provide members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints with the tools necessary to be successful in their callings. We seek out resources that can be used to understand how to serve in the specific callings of the Church that will help those serving to help build the Kingdom of God on the earth.
